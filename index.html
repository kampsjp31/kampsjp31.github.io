Who we are

Jotties is a community of like minded individuals who hang in the garage.

What we do

Come Wednesday, it's Jotties time. Every Wendesday (weather permitting) we hang in the garage. We watch sports, we watch stupid YouTube video, and we drink what ever is in the cooler.     

Whats For dinner?

Thai food, Sultans Market, Bbq, you name it! Each week we either order food or make food.
